WOMEN IN TECH

The tech industry has a significant gender gap, with women being underrepresented. Women face various obstacles, such as a lack of support, limited opportunities, and few role models. Encouraging girls to pursue STEM fields and creating inclusive workplaces can help. More women in tech can benefit the industry in many ways, such as promoting diversity, innovation, and problem-solving. Women have unique skills and perspectives that can improve teamwork and user experience design. Additionally, increasing the number of women in tech can help reduce the gender pay gap. Women in tech earn on average 85% of what their male counterparts make. We need to elevate the voices and experiences of women in tech, highlighting their accomplishments and promoting their work.
